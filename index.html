Welcome to JavaQuest!

JavaQuest is an interactive Java learning platform designed like a game. You can go through levels, earn XP, and master coding step by step. Our friendly mascot will guide you through your journey!

Each level covers key Java concepts: variables, loops, methods, and classes. Visualizations and hints help you learn faster and avoid getting stuck.
